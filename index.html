    Project Description

    DriveNAV is a pioneering technology company that originated as a Mississippi State University senior design engineering project. Our mission is to enhance the lives of the hearing impaired by providing innovative solutions. We focus on developing devices with heads-up displays and haptic feedback to alert users of their surroundings, especially emergency vehicle sounds.

    Learn More

    Technology

    Our cutting-edge technology integrates heads-up displays into our devices, offering a visual representation of surrounding sounds. The haptic feedback provides tactile alerts, ensuring users are notified of emergency vehicle sounds effectively, enhancing their safety and awareness.

    We prioritize user experience by continuously improving our technology to deliver seamless integration and reliable performance. Our commitment to innovation drives us to create intuitive solutions that cater to the specific needs of the hearing impaired community.
